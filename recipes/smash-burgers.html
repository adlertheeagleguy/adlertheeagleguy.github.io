Crispy Smash Burgers

The kind of burger that makes you close your eyes on the first bite.

⏱ 20 min
🍽 4 burgers
🔥 Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 — don't go leaner)
  • 4 slices American cheese (yes, American. trust me)
  • 4 potato buns (Martin's if you can find them)
  • 1 white onion, thinly sliced
  • Dill pickle slices
  • Yellow mustard
  • Salt & black pepper
  • Butter for toasting buns

Instructions

  1. Divide beef into 4 equal balls (~4 oz each). Don't season yet — season the griddle, not the meat.
  2. Heat a cast iron skillet or griddle over high heat until it's screaming hot. You want wisps of smoke.
  3. Butter and toast the buns on the griddle until golden. Set aside.
  4. Place a ball of beef on the hot griddle. Immediately smash it flat with a sturdy spatula or burger press — press HARD, one time, and hold for . Season the top generously with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook without moving for ~ until the edges are deeply browned and crispy. You'll see juice pooling on top — that's the signal to flip.
  6. Flip, immediately lay a slice of cheese on top, and cook . The cheese will melt while the second side crisps.
  7. Stack: bottom bun → mustard → burger patty → pickles → onions → top bun. Serve immediately.
💡 Pro tip: If you want a double, stack two smashed patties. The magic of smash burgers is the crispy crust-to-meat ratio — thin patties = more crust per bite. Don't make thick patties and call it a smash burger.
