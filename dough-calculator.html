🧾 Ingredients

Flour 1248g
Water 808g
Salt 40g
Oil 62g
Yeast 3g
Total Dough 2161g
💧 Cool water to
70°F (21°C)

🥣 Dough Making Instructions:

1. Combine flour, salt, and yeast in a large bowl.
2. Add water and oil, mix until combined.
3. Knead for 10-15 minutes until smooth and elastic.
4. Cover and let rise for 1-2 hours until doubled.
5. Divide into balls, shape, and top as desired.
6. Bake at 500°F (260°C) for 12-15 minutes.
