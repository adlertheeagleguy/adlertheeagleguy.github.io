🧾 Ingredients

Target Dough Ball Size: 255g per ball
Bread Flour 1509g
Water 936g
Yeast 6.0g
Salt 38g
Sugar 30g
Olive Oil 50g
Total Dough 2550g

🥣 Dough Making Instructions:

  1. Cool water to <60°F.
  2. Mix the water and yeast.
  3. Add flour and Olive Oil and mix for 2 minutes.
  4. Keep mixing on low, then add Sugar and Salt.
  5. Mix for 10 more minutes.
  6. Cover the dough with plastic wrap and rest for 1-3 hours.
  7. Divide the dough into portions.
  8. Shape into balls, sealing the seam.
  9. Put the dough in a lightly oiled container.
  10. Cover tightly and refrigerate for 2–4 days. 3 days is ideal.
  11. Let the dough come to room temperature before using.
